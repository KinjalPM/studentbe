Company details:

Get all comapny details:

/v1/company/getallcompany

Search company with name:

/v1/company/companieByName?name=Kinjal

Create New Company:

/v1/company/createnewcompany

Delete a Company will also delete all record connect to company:

/v1/company/deleteByID?id=_idno

Update Company details(patch):

/v1/company/updatethecompany

Replace the Company details (put):

/v1/company/updateonesremain

Records Details:

Get all Records:

/v1/record/getAllRecords

Create New Record which will automatically connect with company:

/v1/record/createnewrecord

Delete a Record:

/v1/record/deletetherecords?id=_idno

Update Record details(patch):

/v1/record/updatetherecord

Replace the Record details (put):

/v1/record/replaceExisting
